CityPay - A Revolutionary Wallet App in Nepal

Kathmandu, Nepal - May 21, 2024 - CityPay, a groundbreaking digital wallet app, is transforming financial transactions in Nepal. By offering seamless top-up, remittance, and payment services, CityPay provides users with the convenience of managing their finances all in one place, backed by robust security measures.

About CityPay

CityPay combines innovation with seamless transactions. As a leader in the financial technology sector, CityPay introduces advanced solutions designed to simplify and enhance users' payment experiences.


Mission and Vision

CityPay's mission is to empower individuals and businesses with secure, efficient, and forward-thinking payment solutions. Committed to making financial transactions accessible to everyone, CityPay aims to foster economic growth and improve the overall quality of life. Looking ahead, CityPay envisions becoming the most preferred payment application for remittance beneficiaries, establishing a nationwide system for easy access to remittance services.


Comprehensive Services

CityPay offers a wide range of services to meet diverse financial needs:


Receive Remittance

Users can seamlessly receive remittances directly into their CityPay wallet, with simple tracking of payment status. Whether from a family member abroad or a supportive friend, CityPay ensures secure and prompt fund delivery


Remittance Services


CityPay facilitates cross-border remittance with seamless transactions and easy payment tracking. Users can receive funds directly into their CityPay wallet and manage finances effortlessly.


Withdraw Cash


CityPay's extensive agent network across Nepal enables users to easily access their funds. The "Find Agents" feature helps locate convenient cash withdrawal points, ensuring smooth transactions in both urban and remote areas.


Track Remittance


CityPay provides an intuitive system for tracking remittances, offering real-time updates from initiation to delivery. This transparency and peace of mind empower users to manage their finances confidently.


Support for Family and Friends

CityPay serves as a tool for strengthening bonds among friends and family. With seamless group transactions and real-time updates, CityPay simplifies financial support and fosters meaningful connections.


Fund Transfer


CityPay enables users to transfer funds effortlessly into their wallets, with easy tracking of payment status. Secure and prompt fund delivery is a hallmark of CityPay's service.


Additional Features
