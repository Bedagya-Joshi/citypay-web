Domestic Remittance from CityPay: Convenient and Secure Money Transfers Across Nepal

In an effort to enhance local remittance transactions and provide our geographically diverse customers with an easy, convenient, and safe money transfer service within Nepal, CityPay has established strategic partnerships with Prabhu Money Transfer Limited (PMT). This collaboration aims to facilitate the delivery of domestic remittance to beneficiaries residing in various parts of the country, ensuring seamless and efficient local remittance within Nepal. This service enables customers to send and receive money to and from any part of the country effortlessly. Through this arrangement, all branches of CityPay can send remittance to numerous locations across Nepal, which will be disbursed by PMT payout locations. Likewise, PMT agents can also send remittance to different branches of CityPay, ensuring a widespread and accessible network for all users.

Effortless Money Transfers Across Nepal

CityPay's domestic remittance service is designed to simplify the process of transferring money within Nepal. Whether you need to send money to family members in remote villages or receive funds from urban centers, CityPay provides a reliable and user-friendly platform. The collaboration with PMT ensures that remittance can be sent and received seamlessly, bridging the gap between urban and rural areas and enhancing financial inclusion.


Comprehensive Network for Wide Coverage

With CityPay, you gain access to a comprehensive network that spans across Nepal. This extensive coverage means that no matter where you or your beneficiaries are located, there is always a nearby CityPay or PMT agent ready to assist. This wide-reaching network ensures that money can be transferred quickly and securely, reaching even the most remote corners of the country.


Secure and Reliable Transactions

Security is paramount when it comes to financial transactions, and CityPay takes this seriously. The domestic remittance service is fortified with advanced security measures to protect your funds and personal information. CityPay employs robust encryption and secure payment gateways, ensuring that every transaction is safe from potential threats. This commitment to security provides users with peace of mind, knowing that their money is in safe hands.


Enhancing Financial Inclusion

CityPay's domestic remittance service plays a crucial role in enhancing financial inclusion across Nepal. By making it easier for people to send and receive money, CityPay is empowering individuals and communities, fostering economic growth, and improving the overall quality of life. This service is particularly beneficial for those in rural areas who may have limited access to traditional banking services, providing them with a convenient alternative to manage their finances.


Conclusion


CityPay is revolutionizing domestic remittance in Nepal, offering a convenient, secure, and efficient way to transfer money within the country. The partnership with Prabhu Money Transfer Limited ensures a broad network and reliable service, making it easier for customers to send and receive money, no matter where they are. Experience the ease and security of domestic remittance with CityPay, and join us in our mission to enhance financial inclusion and support economic growth across Nepal.
